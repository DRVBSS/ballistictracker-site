Frequently asked questions

How do I join the beta?

BallisticTracker is currently in beta through Apple's TestFlight. Tap Join the Beta, install the free TestFlight app from Apple if prompted, then accept the invitation to install BallisticTracker.

What do I need to run it?

An iPhone or iPad running iOS 26 or later. Some features — live weather and range location — work best with an internet connection and Location Services enabled.

Where is my data stored? Is it private?

Your records live on your device and sync privately through your own iCloud account. We don't run servers that hold your data, there are no ads or trackers, and we never sell your information. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do range scoring and DOPE cards work?

Start a session with your firearm, ammo, distance, and conditions. Photograph your target and tap to mark each shot — the App computes your group size and MOA. Generate a DOPE card from that data, then confirm it against real results on future range days to validate your come-ups.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Range sessions, DOPE cards, and records can be exported as polished PDFs you can print or share with a coach.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email support@ballistictracker.com with a short description and, for bugs, the steps to reproduce it. Beta feedback directly shapes what we build next.
