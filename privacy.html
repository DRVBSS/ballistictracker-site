Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

The short version: BallisticTracker has no ads and no third-party trackers, and we never sell your data. The information you enter stays on your device and syncs only through your own private iCloud account — it does not pass through servers operated by us.

This Privacy Policy explains how BallisticTracker (the “App”), published by Vega LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”), handles information when you use the App on your iPhone or iPad. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information the App handles

BallisticTracker is designed to keep your information on your own devices and in your own iCloud account. Depending on the features you use, the App stores:

Information you enter

  • Identity and contact details you choose to add — such as a shooter name, email address, phone number, and physical or range addresses — used to personalize your sessions, locations, and records.
  • Your firearm and shooting data — inventory entries, ammunition, accessories, provenance notes, insurance details, range sessions, shot scores, DOPE cards, and any photos you capture (for example, target photos used for shot marking).

Information used by features

  • Precise location — when you tap “Use Current” in a range session, the App reads your device location to tag where you were shooting. Location is used only for this purpose; it is not linked to advertising and is not used to track you.
  • Weather conditions — when you request current weather, the App uses Apple WeatherKit. To return local conditions, your approximate location is sent to Apple in accordance with Apple's privacy policy.
  • Camera — used only when you choose to photograph a target or item. Photos are stored with your records; they are not uploaded to us.
  • Notifications — if you allow notifications, they are used to support App features such as reminders. You can turn them off anytime in iOS Settings.

This information is collected solely to make the App's features work. It is not used for tracking across other apps or websites, and the App contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

2. Where your data is stored

Your records are stored locally on your device and synced through Apple iCloud (CloudKit) using your personal Apple Account, so your data is available across your own Apple devices. This sync is handled by Apple's infrastructure. Vega LLC does not operate servers that receive or store your App content, and we cannot access the data held in your private iCloud database.

3. Third-party services

The App relies on the following Apple services, each governed by Apple's privacy practices:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — private storage and sync of your records.
  • Apple WeatherKit — current weather conditions for range sessions.

We do not share your information with any other third parties for their own purposes.

4. How we use information

  • To provide and operate the App's features.
  • To respond to you if you contact us for support.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

5. Your choices and control

  • You control Location, Camera, and Notification permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security and in the App's settings.
  • You can delete individual records inside the App.
  • You can remove all App data by deleting the App and removing its data from your iCloud account in iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud.
  • For questions about your data, contact us at the address below.

6. Data retention

Because your data lives in your own device storage and iCloud account, you control how long it is kept. It remains until you delete it or remove it from iCloud.

7. Children

BallisticTracker is intended for adults and is rated 17+. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Security

Your data benefits from the security of your device and your Apple Account, including device passcode/biometric protection and Apple's iCloud security. Please protect your device and Apple Account credentials.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and post the new version on this page.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email support@ballistictracker.com.
